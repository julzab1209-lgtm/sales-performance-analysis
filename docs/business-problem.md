# Business Problem

## Overview

Organizations need quick access to sales data to monitor performance and support business decisions.

This project demonstrates how an interactive Power BI dashboard can help analyze sales across products, countries, and time periods.

---

## Objectives

- Monitor sales performance
- Compare products and countries
- Identify sales trends
- Support data-driven decision-making

---

## Expected Outcome

The dashboard provides a clear and interactive overview of sales data, helping users identify trends and evaluate business performance.
